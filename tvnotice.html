About

Jharkhand: With a vision to usher new dawn of progress and growth, JSP has established a 1.6 Million Tonne Per Annum capacity steel plant at Patratu, Jharkhand. Going forward, the company plans to establish other industrial projects in this region as well.The company has pledged overall development of the community through provision of health services, education, sports, safe drinking water, women empowerment, self-employment and infrastructure development to improve the quality of life of the people in villages.
